GIVE INDIA

is a non profit organisation dedicated to promote a "giving culture" in India. We aspire for a world where all of us come together to create a better and an equal world for everyone. GiveIndia is a ‘philanthropy marketplace’ that helps in bridging the gap between donors and NGOs in an efficient and effective way.A strong "giving" culture where Indians donate 2% of their income every year to give the poor a chance. A vibrant "philanthropy marketplace" to ensure that the most efficient and effective nonprofits get access to the most resources

About Us

GiveIndia is a giving platform that allows you to support a cause of your choice from the NGOs that have been evaluated for transparency and credibility.GiveIndia is India's largest and most trustworthy giving platform for donors. With contributions of over Rs. 3 billion made to our 200+ vetted and trusted nonprofits, our giving community of 1M+ donors has impacted lives of millions of people across 23 states in India. We tell you exactly where your money went and also give you proof of it through donation reports.

How we work

Here is how we ensure that your contribution reaches the people and the organization you support.

1. You select the program that you wish to commit to support

2. You receive a tax exemption receipt via email as soon as your donation transaction is complete.

3. The amount is allocated to the program and nonprofit of your choice

4. We track every donation and ensure that the nonprofit sends us a report on how they utilize donations

5. We give you periodic updates about the people and the program you are helping

When you choose us to be your giving partner, you can rest assured that your contributions do what you want them to do.
